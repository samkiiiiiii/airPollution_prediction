About ARIMA

ARIMA stands for autoregressive integrated moving average

  1. Usally consider the frequency (時段/季度) and time
    Model: ARIMA(P,D,Q)s
  2. consider the change in frequency (時段/季度) and time to predict the future
    Model: ARIMA(P,D,Q)

P

find out the value in the past affect the value now.
ex: the temperature in the past three days are 20 degrees . we can predict the temperature is 20 degree today.

D

The change in the past
ex: the temperature increase rapidly in last three days. the probability of temperature increase today is high.

Q

We can set the error rate equal to the difference we observed in the past
